\noindent Let $(p, q) \in \mathbb{Z} | p \land q = 1 \ and \ \frac{p}{q} = \sqrt{2}$\\
$\Rightarrow p^2 = 2q^2$ \\
$\Rightarrow p^2 | 2$\\
$\Rightarrow p = 1 \ or \ p = 2$
$\Rightarrow p | q $ Contradiction . $\blacksquare$
